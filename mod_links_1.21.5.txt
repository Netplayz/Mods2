-------------------------
- Install Fabric Loader for Minecraft 1.21.5: https://fabricmc.net/use/
- Place all downloaded .jar files into your mods folder:
  C:\Users\YourUsername\AppData\Roaming\.minecraft\mods
- Launch Minecraft using the Fabric profile.
